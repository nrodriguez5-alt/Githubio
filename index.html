Nick Rod

Cuban American independent artist · rapper · producer · songwriter

Short Bio

I’m a Cuban American independent artist and music student focused on building a sustainable career outside traditional industry paths. My work blends rap and melodic songwriting, and I’m interested in how technology and direct fan relationships can support long-term creative independence.

What I’m Working On

Right now I’m focused on releasing music consistently, improving my production and writing, and learning how to build repeatable systems around my art.

What I Care About Right Now

I’m curious about how artists can use emerging technology to stay independent without burning out or chasing hype. I care about ownership, real fan connection, and building things that last instead of optimizing for short-term attention. If you’re interested in where music, culture, and new tools overlap in a more thoughtful way, that’s what I’m exploring.
